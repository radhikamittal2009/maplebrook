About

Welcome to Maplebrook Library, the heart of Ottawa's literary and learning community! We pride ourselves on fostering a love for knowledge through an extensive collection of resources and engaging workshops tailored for children. Our partnership with 20 city schools strengthens our mission to inspire young minds and nurture lifelong learners. Beyond books, Maplebrook Library is a hub for creativity, with interactive programs designed to educate and entertain. Discover a world of possibilities in a space where learning meets imagination!
